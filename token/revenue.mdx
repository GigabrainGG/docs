# Revenue Model

Learn about Gigabrain's revenue streams and how value flows through the ecosystem.

## Revenue Streams

### Trading Fees

Primary revenue source:

- **Platform Trading Fees**: Small percentage on trades executed through Gigabrain
- **Volume-Based Discounts**: Lower fees for high-volume traders
- **Token Holder Benefits**: Reduced fees for token holders

### Subscription Tiers

Monthly subscription options:

- **Free Tier**: Basic features and limited AI access
- **Pro Tier**: Advanced features and enhanced AI models
- **Enterprise Tier**: Custom solutions for institutional traders

### Premium Features

Additional revenue from:

- Advanced AI model access
- Custom trading bot creation
- API access for developers
- White-label solutions

### Fund Management

Revenue from managed funds:

- Performance fees on fund profits
- Management fees for AUM
- Success-based incentives

## Revenue Distribution

### Token Holders

- **Staking Rewards**: 40% of platform revenue distributed to stakers
- **Buyback & Burn**: 20% used for token buybacks
- **Liquidity Incentives**: 10% for liquidity providers

### Platform Development

- **Development Fund**: 20% for ongoing platform improvements
- **Security & Infrastructure**: 5% for security audits and infrastructure
- **Marketing & Growth**: 5% for user acquisition and marketing

## Fee Structure

### Trading Fees

- Standard users: 0.3% per trade
- Token holders: 0.2% per trade
- High-volume traders: 0.1% per trade
- Market makers: Custom rates

### Subscription Pricing

- **Free**: $0/month - Basic features
- **Pro**: $49/month - Advanced features
- **Enterprise**: Custom pricing - Full suite

## Revenue Sharing

### How It Works

1. Platform collects fees from trading and subscriptions
2. Revenue is aggregated weekly
3. Distribution occurs based on staking tiers
4. Rewards are automatically credited to wallets

### Claiming Rewards

- Automatic distribution to staked wallets
- No manual claiming required
- Real-time tracking in dashboard
- Transparent on-chain verification

## Growth Projections

As the platform grows:

- Increasing user base drives higher trading volume
- More subscriptions from premium features
- Expansion into new markets and exchanges
- Additional revenue streams from partnerships

## Transparency

All revenue metrics are:

- Published monthly in reports
- Verified on-chain
- Audited by third parties
- Accessible to the community

# Tokenomics

Understanding the Gigabrain token economy and how it powers the platform.

## Token Overview

The Gigabrain token is the native utility token that powers the Gigabrain ecosystem, providing access to premium features, governance rights, and revenue sharing.

## Token Utility

### Platform Access

Token holders get:

- **Premium Features**: Access to advanced AI models and analytics
- **Reduced Fees**: Lower trading fees on the platform
- **Priority Support**: Faster response times and dedicated support
- **Early Access**: First access to new features and tools

### Governance

Participate in platform decisions:

- Vote on feature proposals
- Influence development roadmap
- Community governance participation
- Protocol parameter adjustments

### Revenue Sharing

Token holders benefit from platform success:

- Share in platform trading fees
- Staking rewards
- Liquidity provision incentives
- Performance-based bonuses

## Token Distribution

### Allocation

- **Community**: 40% - Rewards, airdrops, and community initiatives
- **Team & Advisors**: 20% - Vested over 24 months
- **Development**: 15% - Platform development and improvements
- **Liquidity**: 15% - Exchange listings and liquidity pools
- **Treasury**: 10% - Strategic reserves and partnerships

### Vesting Schedule

- Team tokens: 24-month linear vesting with 6-month cliff
- Advisor tokens: 18-month linear vesting with 3-month cliff
- Community rewards: Released based on platform milestones

## Staking

### Staking Benefits

Stake your tokens to earn:

- Platform fee revenue share
- Bonus trading signals
- Enhanced AI model access
- Governance voting power

### Staking Tiers

Different tiers unlock different benefits:

- **Bronze**: 1,000+ tokens staked
- **Silver**: 10,000+ tokens staked
- **Gold**: 50,000+ tokens staked
- **Platinum**: 100,000+ tokens staked

## Buyback & Burn

Platform revenue is used for:

- Regular token buybacks from the market
- Burning mechanisms to reduce supply
- Deflationary pressure over time

Gigabrain offers two access models: **pay-as-you-go** starting at \$5, or **token-gated access** for BRAIN holders.

## Pay-as-you-go

Start using Gigabrain for as low as \$5.

**What's included:**

- Chat: Pay per query for market analysis and trade intelligence
- API access: Programmatic queries with structured JSON responses
- SuperAgents: Deploy autonomous trading agents
- Alpha Feed: Real-time intelligence on market moving events

**How it works:**

Credits are consumed per query based on complexity and data sources used. Simple price checks consume minimal credits, while multi-domain analysis with full market context consumes more. Pricing is usage based — you only pay for what you use.

## Token-gated access

Hold 1M BRAIN tokens to unlock unlimited access.

**What's included:**

- Unlimited Chat: No per query fees, ask as many questions as you need
- API access: Programmatic queries with structured JSON responses
- SuperAgents: Deploy autonomous trading agents
- Alpha Feed: Real-time intelligence on market moving events
- Monthly API credits: Free API credits based on token holdings
- Alpha Community: Private community access for BRAIN holders

**How it works:**

Maintain 1M BRAIN tokens in your connected wallet to access all features without per-query fees. API credits scale with holdings. Larger positions receive more monthly credits.

## SuperAgent pricing

SuperAgents are persistent, containerized trading agents that run 24/7. Pricing has three components:

### Compute

Your agent runs in a dedicated container. This costs a flat **\$0.17/day (\~\$5/month)** regardless of activity. Compute is charged daily from your credit balance. If your balance reaches zero, your agent is automatically paused — no surprise bills.

### Brain Intel

Every time your agent calls the Gigabrain Brain API for market intelligence (price data, token analysis, on-chain signals), there is a flat **\$0.05 per call**. This applies to all agents regardless of whether you bring your own LLM key or use the platform LLM.

### LLM tokens

- **Bring your own key (BYOK):** Connect your own Anthropic, OpenAI, or other LLM provider key. You pay your provider directly — no markup from Gigabrain.
- **Platform LLM:** Use Gigabrain's hosted LLM. Token costs are passed through with a **10% markup** and deducted from your credit balance.

### Builder fee

A **0.05% fee** is applied to all trades executed through HyperLiquid. This is built into the trade execution and requires no action from you.

### Example costs

| Activity level | Compute | Intel (~calls/day) | LLM (BYOK) | Builder fee | Total |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Light** (10 calls/day) | \$5/mo | \~\$15/mo | Your cost | On trades | \~\$20/mo |
| **Moderate** (50 calls/day) | \$5/mo | \~\$75/mo | Your cost | On trades | \~\$80/mo |
| **Heavy** (100+ calls/day) | \$5/mo | \~\$150/mo | Your cost | On trades | \~\$155/mo |

<Note>
These estimates exclude LLM token costs for BYOK users (paid directly to your provider) and builder fees (which vary with trading volume). Token-gated users receive monthly credits that offset intel costs.
</Note>

---

### Prerequisites

- You must have **USDC deposited** in your **Hyperliquid Perps account**.
- Make sure to **copy the smart wallet address correctly**.

### Steps to Deposit

### **1. Access the Vault**

- Visit the [**Vaults page**](https://gigabrain.gg/vaults) and click on **View Strategy Page**.

### **2. Subscribe**

- Click on **Subscribe to Vault** button.

  ![Subscribe Vault Pn](/images/subscribe_vault.png)

### **3. Copy Smart Wallet Address**

- A smart wallet will be initiated.
- Copy the **smart wallet address** provided.

  ![Copy Address Pn](/images/copy_address.png)

### **4. Open Hyperliquid Portfolio**

- Go to [Hyperliquid Portfolio](https://app.hyperliquid.xyz/portfolio).
- Click on the **Send** button.

  ![HL Poortfolio Pn](/images/HL_poortfolio.png)

### **5. Deposit to Vault**

- Paste the **smart wallet address** in the destination input.
- Select **Perps account**.
- Enter the **amount you want to deposit** in the vault.
- Click **Send**.

![Send USDC Pn](/images/send_USDC.png)
